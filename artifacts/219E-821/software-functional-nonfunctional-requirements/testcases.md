## Verify Functional Modes, Performance Limits, and Non-Functional Behavior

Verify that the software correctly transitions between all defined operational modes and fault modes, enforces the specified performance limits (maximum vehicle speed, torque boundaries, gear transitions), correctly initializes to the defined default/init state, and satisfies non-functional requirements including response time under load, configurability via the service interface, and accurate visualization of system states and error codes on the display.
