## Software Functional & Non-Functional Requirements

Covers all functional software requirements including high-level behavioral descriptions, feature blocks, normal and fault-mode operation, performance limits (speed, torque, modes), as well as non-functional requirements such as usability, reliability, efficiency, changeability, portability, maintainability, and standards compliance. Also includes configurability and parametrization requirements.
