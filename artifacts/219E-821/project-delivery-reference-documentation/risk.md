## Risk: Missing or Outdated Reference Documents Causing Rework

If CAN specifications, data sheets, or additional reference documents are missing, outdated, or not formally listed in the delivery package, downstream engineering (software design, hardware integration, testing) may be based on incorrect assumptions, causing significant rework after customer review. Mitigation requires a document checklist review at each PDP milestone with sign-off confirming that all reference documents are current and accessible.
