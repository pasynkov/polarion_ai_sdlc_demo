## Risk: Incomplete or Incorrect I/O Pin Mapping Causing Electrical Damage

Incorrect or incomplete specification of electrical connections, pin assignments, or signal ranges for input/output components may result in wiring errors during integration, potentially causing electrical damage to hydraulic control hardware, CAN bus failures, or undefined behavior of actuators (pumps, valves, motors). Mitigation requires a peer-reviewed I/O pin table and signal validation tests prior to hardware integration.
